Instructions

Your goal is to match the cards accordingly. The player turns over two cards (one at a time), if they match, they are kept face up and the player can move on to the next two cards. When the player chooses two cards that do not match, they will be returned again in the same position they were in before. The less time and less movements the higher your score.
